Advertisement Creation Guide

Effective Ad Structure

  • Hook: Capture attention immediately with a compelling headline
  • Problem: Identify the pain point or need
  • Solution: Present your product/service as the solution
  • Benefits: Highlight key advantages
  • Proof: Include social proof or evidence (when possible)
  • CTA: End with a clear, action-oriented call to action

Platform-Specific Best Practices

Facebook/Instagram
  • Keep text minimal in images (under 20% of image area)
  • Focus on visually compelling content
  • Use short, direct copy that works without sound
  • Optimal video length: 15-30 seconds
  • Include a clear CTA button
Google Search
  • Headline 1: Up to 30 characters
  • Headline 2: Up to 30 characters
  • Headline 3: Up to 30 characters
  • Description: Up to 90 characters
  • Include keywords in headlines
  • Focus on specificity and relevance
LinkedIn
  • Use professional, benefit-focused language
  • Headline: Up to 150 characters
  • Descriptive text: Up to 2,000 characters
  • Focus on business value and professional outcomes
  • Use statistics and specific results

Ad Copy Tips

  • Lead with the most compelling benefit
  • Use active voice and present tense
  • Address the reader directly using "you" and "your"
  • Create a sense of urgency or exclusivity when appropriate
  • Keep language simple and accessible
  • Use specific numbers and facts rather than generalizations
  • Focus on benefits, not just features
