Social Media Post Best Practices

Platform-specific guidelines:

  • LinkedIn: Professional tone, industry insights, career advice, company updates (1,500-2,000 character limit)
  • Twitter/X: Concise, newsworthy, question-based engagement, trending topics (280 character limit)
  • Facebook: Community focused, stories, questions, videos, longer form content (63,206 character limit but aim for 1-2 paragraphs)
  • Instagram: Highly visual, authentic stories, lifestyle content, behind-the-scenes (2,200 character limit but aim for 1-3 short paragraphs)
  • TikTok: Entertaining, educational, authentic, trending sounds/effects (2,200 character limit but focus on video content)
  • Pinterest: Inspirational, DIY, planning, lifestyle, strong visuals (500 character limit)

Post type best practices:

  • Educational: Lead with a surprising fact, break down complex topics, use numbered lists
  • Promotional: Focus on benefits not features, include social proof, create urgency
  • Engagement: Ask open-ended questions, use polls, invite opinions on trending topics
  • Company News: Share the impact not just the announcement, highlight people involved
  • Industry News: Add your unique perspective, explain why it matters to your audience
  • Event: Create FOMO, highlight key speakers/activities, clear date/time/location
  • User-Generated: Thank the original creator, add your insights, ask for more submissions
  • Storytelling: Use narrative structure, create emotional connection, authentic voice

General tips for all platforms:

  • Start with a hook in the first line (question, surprising fact, bold statement)
  • Break text into short paragraphs with line breaks for readability
  • Use emojis strategically to highlight key points (1-3 per post)
  • Include relevant hashtags (2-5 for most platforms, 10-15 for Instagram)
  • End with a clear, single call-to-action
  • Create alignment between text content and visual elements
  • Respond to comments within 24 hours to boost engagement

Optimal posting times:

  • LinkedIn: Tuesday through Thursday, 9am-12pm
  • Twitter/X: Monday through Friday, 8am-4pm
  • Facebook: Tuesday through Thursday, 1pm-4pm
  • Instagram: Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, 11am-1pm
  • TikTok: Tuesday through Saturday, 7pm-9pm
  • Pinterest: Saturday and Sunday, 8pm-11pm
